Open-Source projects

CNN model weight compresion

Goal: Due to the constant demand and need for AI technologies in different fields, and in some of those fields we do not have enough memory to hold all the weights of the CNN model to use it in its full potential. I started researching on all of those networks starting from Alex Net to Google mobile net to many other fascinating networks and then made our own version which got to 0.13 MB memory footprint on Kaggel cats and dogs. digit dataset with 70% accuracy.

Tech-stack: Python, Keras, Tenorflow, Jupytier notebook.

Year: 2019

Country: Syria
